Anatomy Diagrams - Labeling Guide

Nephron (Kidney) Diagram

  • 1. Glomerulus - Network of capillaries where filtration begins
  • 2. Bowman's capsule - Cup-shaped structure surrounding glomerulus
  • 3. Proximal convoluted tubule - First twisted section after Bowman's capsule
  • 4. Loop of Henle (descending limb) - Downward loop section
  • 5. Loop of Henle (ascending limb) - Upward loop section
  • 6. Distal convoluted tubule - Second twisted section
  • 7. Collecting duct - Final tube where urine collects
  • 8. Afferent arteriole - Blood vessel bringing blood to glomerulus
  • 9. Efferent arteriole - Blood vessel carrying blood away from glomerulus
  • 10. Peritubular capillaries - Network of capillaries around tubules

Breast Anatomy Diagram

  • 1. Nipple - Protruding structure at center of breast
  • 2. Areola - Darker circular area surrounding nipple
  • 3. Mammary glands (lobules) - Milk-producing glands
  • 4. Lactiferous ducts - Tubes carrying milk to nipple
  • 5. Lactiferous sinus - Milk storage area near nipple
  • 6. Adipose tissue (fat) - Fatty tissue giving breast its size/shape
  • 7. Pectoralis major muscle - Chest muscle beneath breast
  • 8. Ribs - Bones of chest wall
  • 9. Suspensory ligaments (Cooper's ligaments) - Support structures
